Gourmet Twizzlers

This is the best recipe you will ever make!

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Corn Syrup
  • Natural Cherry Extract
  • Naural Strawberry Extract
  • Fruit Concentrate

Directions

  1. Combine sugar, syrup, cherry/strawberry extracts, and fruit concentrate
  2. Heat in saucepan
  3. Bring to boil and clip a candy thermometer to side of pot
  4. Cook to 240 F
  5. Transfer to greased surface
  6. Trim to six-inch lengths and place in rows
  7. Twist Twizzlers, lengthening ropes then trim edges with a bench scrape
  8. Let sit at room temperature until set
